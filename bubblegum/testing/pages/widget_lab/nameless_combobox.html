Nameless combobox (no accessible name)

The trigger is role="combobox" with no aria-label, aria-labelledby, or associated <label>. Per ARIA, a combobox does not take its name from content, so it surfaces as a bare combobox in the accessibility tree. Bubblegum's nameless-combobox fallback resolves it by role + uniqueness when the step signals a dropdown (e.g. "Open the fruit dropdown").
